Ayaz "nAts" Akhmetshin Player History

Career Timeline

  • 2020 — early career & regional teams 2020
    Started competing in Valorant and joined regional squads before signing for larger orgs.
  • 2021 — Gambit Esports, Masters Berlin Champion 2021
    Key breakthrough: nAts was part of Gambit’s roster that won VALORANT Stage 3 Masters Berlin in 2021, a major LAN title that raised his profile internationally.
  • 2022 — Signed by Team Liquid Late 2022
    He later moved to Team Liquid, where he continued as an IGL and contributed to the team’s performances in EMEA and global events.
  • 2023–2025 — Continued competition & recent news 2023–2025
    nAts remained active on the pro circuit with Team Liquid; recent coverage also discussed visa issues surrounding attendance at Masters Toronto (2025).
